What is the price variance calculator?
The price variance calculator is a tool that computes the result of an unpredicted change in the direct material cost that would disturb the overall cost.
How to calculate price variance?
To calculate price variance you should use the following formula:
price variance = (standard price - actual price) * actual quantity
